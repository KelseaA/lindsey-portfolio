Projects

Writing

I wrote "One Year On, Is Theaster Gates Reimagining the Modern Museum at the Stony Island Arts Bank?" for ArtSlant, a national arts magazine with a contemporary focus.

I wrote the ad copy the museum used to publicize the most successful exhibition in its history, a retrospective of musician and art icon David Bowie.

"I regularly write for the Museum of Contemporary Art Chicago's blog, MCA DNA."

Editing

I copy edited and helped manage the production of Kerry James Marshall: Mastry, an artist book published by the MCA in 2016.

Since joining the MCA, I have served as editor-in-chief of its tri-annual art magazine, shaping each issue's content and managing its production.

I edited the content for this educational video game (launched by McGraw-Hill to help high school students improve their reading and writing abilities), supervising twelve freelance writers over the course of the project.
